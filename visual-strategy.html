Execution Strategy

Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)

Build landing pages, activate Charlie's owned audience, launch cold email to healer list (150k), set up referral program via Viral Loops.

Phase 2: Paid Acquisition (Week 3-8)

Meta ads to all 5 audiences, Google Ads (SEM), retargeting, nurture sequences in Loops. Track every conversion with PostHog.

🤖 Phase 3: Automation (Week 8-12)

Agents take over Patient funnel. 95% autonomous. Email, retargeting, matching, retention all automated. Humans review escalations only.

Phase 4: Optimization (Month 4+)

PostHog feedback loops. A/B test copy. Shift budget to top-performing channels. Scale healer/patient matching. Referral program fuels growth.
