# 🚀 Operations Pages Quick Start

## What's New?

Four brand-new production-ready pages have been added to your ENNIE Marketing Hub:

### 1️⃣ Metrics Dashboard (`metrics.html`)
**See all your numbers in real-time:**
- Total spend, conversions, ROI
- Charts: Conversions over time, Spend by platform, Funnel comparison
- Funnel breakdown with conversion rates
- Cost per acquisition by channel
- Revenue & payout details

**Access:** Click "Metrics Dashboard" in Operations section

---

### 2️⃣ Campaign Calendar (`calendar.html`)
**Visualize all your campaigns:**
- Full calendar view with color-coded campaigns
- Timeline view (sorted by date)
- Switch between Month & Timeline views
- Click any campaign to see details
- Status badges (Active/Paused/Scheduled)

**Features:**
- 🟢 Healer campaigns = Green
- 🟣 Patient campaigns = Purple
- 🟠 Referral campaigns = Orange
- 🔴 Untrained campaigns = Red
- 🔵 Owned campaigns = Blue

**Access:** Click "Campaign Calendar" in Operations section

---

### 3️⃣ Integration Status (`integrations.html`)
**Monitor your API connections:**
- See which integrations are connected (Google Ads, Meta, TikTok, PostHog, ElevenLabs)
- Check last sync times
- Test connections
- Connect new integrations
- View what data is being synced from each

**Status at a glance:**
- 🟢 Connected: Google Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
- 🔴 Disconnected: TikTok Ads, PostHog Analytics, ElevenLabs Voice

**Access:** Click "Integration Status" in Operations section

---

### 4️⃣ Team & Settings (`team-settings.html`)
**Manage your team and configuration:**

**Team Tab:**
- View all team members (Charlie, Jake, Jess, Felix)
- Add new team members
- Edit roles and permissions
- Remove team members

**Settings Tab:**
- Save API keys for Google Ads & Meta
- Configure webhooks for real-time events
- Control notification preferences
- Set organization name & timezone

**Access:** Click "Team & Settings" in Settings section

---

## How to Use

### From Master Hub
1. Open `master-hub.html` in your browser
2. Click any of the new cards in **Operations & Analytics** section
3. Or use the sidebar navigation:
   - Operations → Metrics Dashboard
   - Operations → Campaign Calendar  
   - Operations → Integration Status
   - Settings → Team & Settings

### Direct Access
Open these files directly in your browser:
- `metrics.html` - View live metrics
- `calendar.html` - Manage campaigns
- `integrations.html` - Check API status
- `team-settings.html` - Manage team
