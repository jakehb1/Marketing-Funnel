🤖 Ad Operations Automation

Growth Agent + Voice Agent + Paid Ads Agent autonomously manage multi-platform ad campaigns across Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok, and Pinterest. The system continuously A/B tests, optimizes spend, and feeds results back into the funnel.

Facebook/Instagram

Budget: £12k/mo
Audiences: Chronic pain groups, wellness interests, lookalikes
Agent: Paid Ads Agent cycles through 5 ad variations every 3 days

Google Ads

Budget: £15k/mo
Keywords: chronic pain relief, energy healing, alternative medicine
Agent: Bid optimization + keyword expansion daily

TikTok

Budget: £8k/mo
Creative: Viral hooks, user testimonials, Charlie clips
Agent: Tests 10 variations, pauses underperformers

Pinterest

Budget: £5k/mo
Pins: Wellness, self-care, healing journey content
Agent: Auto-cycles designs weekly

Daily Agent Loop

06:00 UTC: Paid Ads Agent checks PostHog metrics from yesterday
06:15 UTC: Stops underperformers (<2% CTR), scales winners (>4% CTR)
06:30 UTC: Voice Agent generates 3 new ad copy variations
07:00 UTC: New variations deployed to active campaigns
Evening: Humans (Jess/Felix) review performance, approve budget shifts, suggest creative angles
Daily: Reports sent to PostHog + Growth Agent for next day optimization
